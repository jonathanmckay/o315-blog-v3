# About Me

I'm Jonathan McKay. I've been keeping a journal since 1999, and this site is a curated selection of entries that might interest the curious.

## What You'll Find Here

Essays and reflections on:
- **Technology & Work**: Stories from Facebook, OpenAI, GitHub/Microsoft, and other tech companies
- **International Experience**: Living and working in China, Saudi Arabia, and serving in the U.S. Foreign Service
- **Language Learning**: The journey of learning Mandarin Chinese
- **Parenting**: Raising three kids (Theo, Ren, and Aurora)
- **Adventure**: Motorcycle trips, travel, and the occasional wreck
- **Philosophy**: Stoicism, mindfulness, and making sense of life

## Background

I've worked at the intersection of technology, policy, and international affairs. My career has taken me from the U.S. State Department to Silicon Valley and back again. I speak Mandarin, enjoy long motorcycle rides, and practice Zenstoic philosophy.

## About This Site

These entries are drawn from over 4,300 journal entries spanning 1999-present. They're presented largely as written, with minimal editing. The goal is simple: tell my story, if only to myself, but also let the curious get to know me.

If you'd like to reach out, you can find me on [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/jomckay) or [GitHub](https://github.com/jomckay).
