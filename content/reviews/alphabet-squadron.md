2nd book of 2020

When I was a kid, the X-Wing series was perfect for my imagination. After devouring a few of the books, I could spend entire evenings just imagining an X-Wing dogfight in my mind. I was curious what a new-generation X-Wing series would look like. 

The book was fine, and I was amused at how much time the author spends focused on bureaucratic wrangling, and leadership posturing within the team. I’m not complaining, this is  my jam. Yet no detail or character was  memorable enough for me to recall 6 months on. I’ll pass on the sequel.
