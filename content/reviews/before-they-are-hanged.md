** Middle Book Syndrome ** 

It doesn't feel like that much happens. Characters grow, but I also want some plot!   

Glokta starts and ends in the capital city.

Logen Ninefingers and his crew start and end without the Seed.

The Northmen and the Union start and end the book on the front foot. 

To quote the Navigator, it's the journey that counts.
