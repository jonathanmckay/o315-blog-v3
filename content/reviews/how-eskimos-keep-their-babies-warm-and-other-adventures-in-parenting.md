83rd book of 2020. Parenting Short Stories. 

Eskimos keep their babies by putting them in the same jacket that Mom is in.  Want more details? Read another book, because this book doesn't have them. 

If this is a 'tour of Global practices' I think a cruiseship at port is the correct analogy. The author seems to be pulling mainly from secondary sources, with no science, little comparative analysis, and no commentary that makes the book worth reading. 

Only two things I took away from this: Try a body wrap, and use the 'try it twice' rule for picky eaters.
