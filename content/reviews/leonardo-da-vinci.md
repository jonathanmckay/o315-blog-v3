I thought I would have liked this book. I’m interested in the person and the era. However the frequent lapses into comparing Leonardo to other innovators, art critique, and other information kept making me lose my focus. It’s been sitting in the queue for about 4 months and I still haven’t had any motivation to get back to it. 

Bailing.
