This is the 'surgeon generals warning' of international studies, and I'm astounded I managed to get a degree in Near Eastern Languages and Civilizations without this being required reading. 

Many of the points ring true, and the book is worth reading eve~if you already k~wthe general thesis. 

Only reason it's not 5 stars is that it was a tough book to get through. The language was dense and the author assumes a lot of prior knowledge that I often didn't have.
