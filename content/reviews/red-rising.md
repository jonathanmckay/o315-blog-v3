I was tempted by a tagline calling this book a combination of Hunger Games, Ender's Game, and Game of Thrones. This book is not like Game of Thrones, and making such a comparison isn't good for Red Rising. The worldbuilding was shallow and the politics dubious. 

Yet, like Ender's Game or Hunger Games, once the games began, it was easy to keep the pages turning. As far as pure entertainment goes, this is probably the best book I've read this year.  I appreciated the tone of anger and guilt that ran through the whole novel, and have already started in on the second book.
