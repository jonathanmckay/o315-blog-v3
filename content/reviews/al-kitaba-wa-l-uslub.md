Intermediate in vocabulary but basic in style and grammar. Save one page, does no analysis on style past the sentence level. Most of the book is spent on tense, and little time is given to finer points of explanation. Most of the exercises consist of repeating in writing what the book has already written. There are annoying typos and unhelpful repetition abounds. 

I'm not sure I would recommend this book to anyone. Spend some real time with the Al-Kitaab series or read Ryding's Arabic grammar reference for grammar.
