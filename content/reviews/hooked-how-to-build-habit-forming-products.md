Meh. 

Not as good as Nudge, even Habit had more depth. the focus on product was nice, but I felt like the author was trying really hard to fit examples to his framework rather than letting the studies speak for themselves. That being said, I wouldn't feel bad recommending this to somebody who is just getting started with ux but didn't want to go in depth.
