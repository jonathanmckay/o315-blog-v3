tl;dr: Go watch Silicon Valley instead. 

First: [ Read this.](https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/1603732582?book_show_action=false&from_review_page=1) Choice quote: "The author, however, continually expresses his disappointment for all the crassest and clueless reasons. He was only in it for the money, but he still felt that somehow, he just wasn't getting the respect he so richly deserved just for showing up. And being a white male." 

Lyons is mystified why his bosses dislike him after his public takedown of the company CEO goes viral, and tries to go over their heads with his pet project. He describes his coworkers as "beer drinking shitheads." He plays office politics poorly, then cries to the reader when his coworkers play office politics back. 

He is at his best when he talks about Silicon Valley culture as a whole, such as his description of Marc Benioff as the 'bulshitter in chief', but listening to his personal experiences is just as bad as reading [b:Behind the Cloud: The Untold Story of How Salesforce.com Went from Idea to Billion-Dollar Company-And Revolutionized an Industry|6659874|Behind the Cloud  The Untold Story of How Salesforce.com Went from Idea to Billion-Dollar Company-And Revolutionized an Industry|Marc Benioff|https://d.gr-assets.com/books/1347488145s/6659874.jpg|6854634].
