**A Predictable End... of the World** 

Not nearly as interesting as Sea of Rust. 

Especially for those that have read Sea of Rust, there's little point in this book. The hope would be one about near-future tactics, but that is something that books like The Last Soldier covered in more detail.  The transformation from nanny into tactical paranoid bot seemed interesting but underdeveloped.
