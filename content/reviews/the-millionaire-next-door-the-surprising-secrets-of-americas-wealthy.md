Not a complex book. Nothing really new, but even if it's thesis can be summed up in one 4 words (spend below your means) it's good to be reminded of that fact. Fascinating also to be reminded of how wealth in America does not follow the contours of consumption and just generally good advice. 

Somewhat dated by this point.
