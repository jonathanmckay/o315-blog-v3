The inline pinyin is annoying, the translations are sloppy, but this is still the best graded reader currently available. Rather than inventing stories with a limited vocabulary, the authors simplified real stories for academic use. As such, the series deals with subjects that Chinese authors want to write about, and is much better for it. 

The stories are interesting, the language is not too advanced, I would recommend this to any but the most beginning Chinese language students.
