Too much of a page turner. The premise -- the capital under siege by 3 competing armies after a successful rebellion -- is probably the best premise for a fantasy page turner that I've yet encountered. But this is the literary equivalent of a summer blockbuster, all action, little substance. 

** 3 stars **
