With a few graceful red lights flickering into the waves, Vo sucks the reader into a world that is familiar to readers of Asian history (or [ fantasy ](https://www.goodreads.com/review/edit/48727813)), but tinged with a velvet sheen of magic that only Vo brings. Far too short for my liking, but that's just another way of saying that I'll definitely read through the novella series.
