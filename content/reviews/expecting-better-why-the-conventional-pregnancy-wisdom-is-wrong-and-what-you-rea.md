Didn't know the first thing about pregnancy, and I've been on a kick with books based on studies for everyday health topic (How not to Diet, Why we Sleep), so this seemed interesting. *Some nausea is good
*1/1500 chance of bacteria infection , wash all veggies
*fish:  omega-3s are good, mercury is bad. Eat small ones. 
*1/700 down syndrome, test in 1st trimester
*take folic acid
