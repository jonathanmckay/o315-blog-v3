**Un-Noteworthy ** 

Do you take notes? Do you reference those notes? 

If yes to both, don't read this book. Go to Tiago's website instead and see if you find anything interesting. Even the [youtube channel ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WTg3fCr1WaY) has more actionable information. 

In the span of 272 pages, all I got was the PARA (Projects, Areas, Research, Archive) framework for top level organization of notes. Useful, but less actionable than I expected, considering I already organize files by area and have long since maintained an archive folder. The rest of the notes I took from this book were either comments on my own note-taking system, or mental eye-rolls at the poor quality writing.

**11th book of 2023 **
